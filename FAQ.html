How To Use The Forum Services

To use our services, you will need to register an account with us. You will need to go through the account registration process and provide the necessary info requested. After registration, log into your account with the username and password set and you can start using our forum services.

What are the community guidelines

The community guidelines are as stated here:
1)Be nice and polite to one another
2)No hate speech, racism or any that are targeted to a specific group of people or an individual
3)No malicious link or solution that will cause another harm
4)The community can be change from time to time and however we see fit to the situation
5)Account in violating the guideline will be ban.

Issue Related to Accounts

If you face any issues related to your accounts, you can contact us through our email address and state in the followings:
1)Your Name
2)Your Username
3)Your Issue
We will get back to you through the email address you have used to send us.

Pertaining to Privacy Matters

The threads and comments you posted are your own responsibility. We strongly discourage any sharing of personal information any personal information shared in the threads and comments are on you own accord. The forum does not hold any responsibility to the disclose personal information.

WE DO NOT ENCOURAGE THE SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION OR SENSITIVE DATA THAT CAN LINK BACK TO YOU.
